## Translation-patcher acknowledgements

Fragmenter's ISO patch workflow was informed by the public behavior and
documentation of Vi Ness's FragmentUpdater and the tellipatch community
workflow. Their executables, private Google credentials, and unlicensed source
code are not bundled into Fragmenter.

Translation work and patch research should retain the credits supplied by the
patch pack's maintainers. Tellipatch 4.0 credits PLM, Princess Alice (Telli),
Dau, Emuz, Enlike, Nyao, Vector, Vi Ness, Xithyl_kykori, and Zackmon. Its
special thanks list names 1UP, Alkalime, Bison, Dash, Erroneous, Esor, Falions,
Harrito, NCDyson, rlstine, Snownami, Robert Coldbird, and the Netslum community.

Tellipatch's `fragment_patcher` distribution includes an MIT license naming
biribiribiri (2019). Fragmenter bundles the unmodified Tellipatch v3.8 XDelta
payload, a compressed export of the live game-line translation sheet, and a
source/target-hash-bound Fragment 4.0 differing-range completion resource for
its native offline applicator. The completion resource contains neither source
ISO, complete game files, nor a reference 4.0 ISO. Fragmenter does not bundle
the old installer, patcher executables, private credentials, .NET runtime, or
ImgBurn. Translation and patch-content credit remains with the Tellipatch
contributors named above.

- Translation project: https://github.com/Tellilum/.hack-fragment-Definitive-Translation
- v3.8 release: https://github.com/Tellilum/.hack-fragment-Definitive-Translation/releases/tag/v3.8
